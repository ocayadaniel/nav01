About Us

KWO GULU, meaning "Gulu Lifestyles," is an innovative project that places dance at the heart of cultural expression and tourism in Northern Uganda. Produced by The Floor Motion, a youth-led collective dedicated to promoting arts, culture, and tourism, this event aims to revitalize cultural heritage, foster local tourism, and empower young creatives. Under the theme “Dance Heritage and Tourism,” KWO GULU seeks to celebrate the uniqueness of the Ac holi/Luo culture while positioning dance as a transformative tool for community engagement and e conomic development.
